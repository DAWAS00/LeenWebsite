Relaxation Techniques for Kid

Hi there, little hero.

If you’re feeling scared or your heart is beating fast, that’s okay.
Let’s take a moment to breathe together.

Breathe in… 1, 2, 3…
And out… 1, 2, 3…

Imagine a safe place
maybe a beach, a tree, or someone you love.
Put your hand on your heart. Feel it? That’s your strength.
You are brave. You are not alone.
And it’s okay to ask for help. We’re with you.
