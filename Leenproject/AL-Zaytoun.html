Our Story

The project explores the psychological effects of war sounds, like bombings, on children in Gaza. It highlights the rise in panic attacks due to constant trauma and calls for urgent psychological support and coping strategies to reduce fear and anxiety during conflict.

Our Team

We are a passionate team of three university students Leen and Bayan
studying Computer Science and Dana study Software Engineering.
United by our dedication to supporting the children of Gaza.

The Goals Of The Website

- Provide psychological support to children Gaza through expert collaboration.
- Create a safe space for children to express their feelings.
- Help children build long-term emotional strength and resilience.
