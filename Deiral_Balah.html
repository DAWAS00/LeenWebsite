The(5-4-3-2-1)grounding technique

5 name things you see.
4 name things you feel.
3 name things you hear.
2 name things you smell.
1 name thing you taste.

After experiencing crime, you may feel overwhelmed or anxious.
A grounding technique is an exercise that can help you to focus on the
present moment. It can help you to relax, to feel calmer or safer,
and can help if you feel distressed or if you're in a moment of panic.
